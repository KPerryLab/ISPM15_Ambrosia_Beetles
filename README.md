#ISPM15-Ambrosia-Beetles
Repository for Jessi's masters research.
Title: The thermal biology of ambrosia beetles and its implications for forest biosecurity                                                
Correspondance: jessiraubenolt@gmail.com

Summary: Data to determine the upper lethal temperatures of three species of ambrosia beetles using a heated water bath and thermocouples. Also testing the heat buffering effects of ambrosia beetle fungal symbionts during ISPM-15 heat treatments using thermal cameras, thermocouples, a gradually heated oven.

Metadata:                                                                                                                                 
GLMSurvival_RawData                                                                                                                       
Date: In MM/DD/YYYY format                                                                                                                
Host_species: Tree host species, "Beech" = American Beech, "Red Maple" = Red Maple                                                        
Temp: Assigned temperature the specimens are removed from the water bath at                                                               
Couple: Thermocouple labels (e.g., "5A" = thermocouple slot 5 on data logger A)                                                           
Slot: Test tube rack labels (e.g., "X1" = Slot 1 of row X on test tube rack)                                                              
Species: Beetle species, "A. maiche" = Anisandrus maiche, "X. germanus" = Xylosandrus germanus, "X. crassiusculus" = Xylosandrus          crassiusculus                                                                                                                             
Lifestage: "Adult" and "Larvae"                                                                                                           
Survival_0min: Assess survival immedately after removal from water bath, and so on. (1 = alive, 0 = dead)                                 

FungalPlateAnalysis_RawData                                                                                                               
Species: Fungal species, "A. cleistominuta" = Ambrosiella cleistominuta, "A. roeperi" = Ambrosiella roeperi, "A. grosmanniae" = Ambrosiella grosmanniae                                                                                                                   
Plate: Assigned replicate number to individual plates                                                                                     
Point: Number of random points assigned to each plate (15 per area, per plate = 30 total per plate)                                       
Area: Area of plate designated as fungal colony (Fungi) or bare agar (Agar)                                                               
Temp: Temperature recordings associated with each random point on plate                                                                   

FungalBoltAnalysis_RawData                                                                                                                
Bolt = Small section of tree branch and/or trunk                                                                                          
Number: Assigned replicate number to individual bolts                                                                                     
Species: Fungal species, "A. cleistominuta" = Ambrosiella cleistominuta, "A. roeperi" = Ambrosiella roeperi, "A. grosmanniae" = Ambrosiella grosmanniae                                                                                                                   
Temp: The temperature points of the oven                                                                                                  
Recorded: Temperatures recorded by thermocouples of the internal portion of the bolts at set oven temps                                   
Diameter.in: Diameter measurements of bolts from inch ruler converted to decimal form                                                     
Diameter.cm: Diameter measurements of bolts converted to centimeters from inches, in decimal form
